THINGS TO DO ON A RAINY DAY AT HOME

Don't know what to do on a rainy day at home? Here are some activities for you and your family!

1. Movie Marathon

During busy days, it's hard to watch shows/movies. But rainy days are the perfect excuse. You can pop some popcorn, choose a show/movie to watch, dim the lights, and get cozy on your couch.

2. Play Board Games

Everybody has a board game that's been lying around the house, this is the day you play it. Sticking to classic board games like Monopoly is fine too. Get competitive!

3. A Cup Of Coffee And A Book

Nothing is more relaxing than a book, a cup of coffee, and the sound of the rain. Find a quiet spot, get your book and your coffee, and enjoy the moment.

4. Try A New Recipe

If you like baking or cooking you can try a new recipe or you can stick with your favorite. (Who can resist chocolate cookies on a rainy day?)

5. Take A Nap

If you are too tired to do anything or just want to rest get cozy and take a nap. The sound of the raindrops can create a great backdrop for your peaceful snooze.

Goodbye!
